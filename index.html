About me

A wanderer
with a camera

I'm a travel photographer based wherever the next flight takes me. For the past eight years I've been chasing light across six continents — from the silk-road cities of Central Asia to the fjords of Norway — always searching for the quiet, unguarded moment that tells a place's true story.

Photography, for me, is about slowing down. It's the discipline of actually looking — at texture, at people, at the way afternoon light turns a dusty street into something luminous.
